# Little Store — Future Vision

Little Store is envisioned as an ecommerce + customer intelligence system.

## North Star

- customers
- funnels
- products
- operations

## Potential capabilities
- storefront
- catalog and CMS
- campaigns and promotions
- CRM and segmentation
- order tracking and fulfillment
- analytics and funnel insights
- AI-assisted merchandising and copy
- Learn surface for merchants
