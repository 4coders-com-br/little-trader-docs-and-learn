# Little Business — Future Vision

> Future vision document.
>
> This is not current product scope. It describes a possible future member of the Little family.

## Purpose

Little Business is envisioned as a serious operating system for modern businesses across:

- commerce
- services
- consulting
- education
- industry
- internal operations

## North Star

Help an operator run a real business with:
- clarity
- inspectability
- AI assistance
- operational discipline
- strong workflows

## Potential capabilities
- CRM
- pipeline and deals
- service delivery workflows
- invoicing and receivables
- CMS and internal knowledge
- marketing and social media operations
- inventory, procurement, and operational analytics
- AI mentor and wizards
- Learn surface for operators
- REPL / execution surface for advanced teams

## Shared Little-family inheritance
- data-driven architecture
- immutable history where it matters
- local-first UX where useful
- MCP and AI-assisted workflows
- rules engine
- strong DevOps
- rich UI
- learnability as part of product

## Status
Future vision only.
